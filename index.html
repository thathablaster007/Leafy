What Are Microgreens?

Microgreens are the tender young shoots of edible plants,harvested just days after germination,packed with intense flavour,vibrant colour and concentrated nutrients.These miniature greens are the perfect fusion of beauty,taste and wellness-a simple way to add freshness and vitality to every meal.
